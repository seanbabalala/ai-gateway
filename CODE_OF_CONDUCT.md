# Code Of Conduct

SiftGate follows a simple contributor covenant:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but correct harmful behavior directly.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work.
- Do not harass, threaten, insult, or discriminate.
- Do not post secrets, private prompts, private responses, or credentials.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who make the project unsafe for others.
